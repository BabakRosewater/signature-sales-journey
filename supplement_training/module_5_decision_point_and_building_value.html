The Affordability Plan

Cam Clark Hyundai • Sales Team Training

Core Training Theme

“Customers are not objections. Customers are thinking through real affordability concerns. Our job is to show them we understand those concerns and can help them make a smart decision.”

The Golden Rule

Don’t sell a payment. Build an affordability plan.

What Customers Are Asking

  • “Can I afford the payment?”
  • “Is this the right time?”
  • “Am I getting real value?”
  • “Can I trust this dealership?”
  • “Will this vehicle protect me from future costs?”

Our Message

“Joe, one thing I want to touch on is this: our sales process is designed to help you make a confident decision.”

Why Cam Clark Hyundai Helps

These are the factual pillars we use to build an affordability plan.

Cam Clark Price

No games, no back-and-forth. Absolute transparency.

Unbeatable Warranty

10-year/100,000-mile coverage helps protect against future repair uncertainty.

Fuel-Efficient Options

Nearly 50% of our inventory is hybrid, actively helping lower the cost of ownership.

Payment-Focused

We don't dictate terms; we help structure the deal around their specific budget.

Trade-in Support

We help customers understand their equity clearly and honestly.

Transparent Process

Less pressure, more facts. Empowering the customer to decide.

Remember: Affordability is Subjective

For one guest, it is the lowest payment. For another, it is the lowest rate. For another, it is more money down, better trade value, shorter term, warranty protection, or lease flexibility. Find out what it means to them.

Opening Line

“Let’s build this around what affordability means to you.”

When PAYMENT is the concern:

“Let’s look at the structure, not just the number.”

When RATE is the concern:

“Sometimes the lowest rate is best. Sometimes the rebate creates the better overall fit.”

When REBATE is the concern:

“The biggest rebate is not always the best plan. Let’s compare it.”

When LEASE may help:

“Leasing may give you more flexibility and a lower monthly commitment.”

When customer is UNSURE:

“That’s exactly why we compare the options side by side.”

Closing Line

“Our goal is to help you leave with a plan that makes sense, not pressure you into a decision.”
