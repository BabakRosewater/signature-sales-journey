## Manager observation checklist
<div class="coach-check">
  <p><strong>During live observation, inspect:</strong></p>
  <ol>
    <li>Did the salesperson recap discovery before showing vehicles?</li>
    <li>Did they show 2–3 best-fit options instead of flooding the guest?</li>
    <li>Did each feature connect to a benefit?</li>
    <li>Did they identify the feedback type before responding?</li>
    <li>Did the turn feel like support instead of pressure?</li>
  </ol>
  <p><strong>Result:</strong> Clear / Partially clear / Needs coaching</p>
</div>
