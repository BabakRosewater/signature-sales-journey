# 4.0 Scripts and Word Tracks

## 4.1 The Empathy + Clarity Opener
“Totally fair. This is a big decision. My job is to make it feel simple and comfortable—no pressure.”

---

## 4.2 Reflect + Validate + Confirm (RVC)
**Reflect:** “So you want something reliable for the long haul.”  
**Validate:** “That makes total sense.”  
**Confirm:** “Did I capture that right?”

---

## 4.3 The ‘Why Ladder’ (Find the Emotional Driver)
“Can I ask—what makes that important to you?”  
(Then one more step)
“And what would it change for you if you had that?”

---

## 4.4 Values Bridge (Need → Feeling)
Need: “Safety” → Feeling: “Peace of mind”  
Need: “AWD” → Feeling: “Confidence”  
Need: “Space” → Feeling: “Less stress / more ease”  
Need: “Tech” → Feeling: “Control / convenience”

**Script**
“Because you mentioned {need}, this helps give you {feeling}—especially in {real-life moment}.”

---

## 4.5 Future Pacing Prompts (Use During Walkaround/Test Drive)
- “Picture your morning routine—how would this change it?”
- “On a winter day, what matters most to you while driving?”
- “When you’re tired after a long day, what features would you appreciate?”
- “If you took a road trip this weekend, what would you want to feel?”

---

## 4.6 The ‘Shared Mission’ Phrase
“Let’s make sure we choose something that fits your life—not just something that looks good on paper.”

---

## 4.7 Reassurance Without Pressure
“It’s completely okay to take your time. I’ll guide the process and keep it simple.”

---

## 4.8 Emotional Close (End of Phase)
“Based on what you told me, this seems to check the boxes that matter most—especially {top value}.  
How close does this feel to what you had in mind?”
