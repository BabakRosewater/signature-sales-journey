# 5.0 Role-Play Lab (Manager-Led)

## Setup (Groups of 3)
- Consultant
- Guest
- Observer/Coach

Rotate every 7–8 minutes.

---

## Drill 1: RVC (Reflect / Validate / Confirm)
**Goal:** Use RVC 3 times in 2 minutes (no sounding robotic).

Guest prompts:
- “I’m nervous about buying again.”
- “I’ve had bad experiences at dealerships.”
- “I don’t want to waste my time.”

Score:
- Natural tone (1–5)
- Guest comfort increased (1–5)

---

## Drill 2: Why Ladder
**Goal:** Ask 2 “why” layers without interrogating.

Guest says:
- “I need something safe.”
- “I care about reliability.”
- “Payment matters.”

Must include:
- Permission ask
- Two-step why ladder
- One values bridge

---

## Drill 3: Future Pacing (Real-life scene)
**Goal:** Make the guest visualize ownership (specific and calm).

Pick one scenario:
- winter commute
- school drop-off
- road trip
- parking at work
- weekend adventure

Must include:
- “Picture…” language
- one sensory detail (warm, quiet, smooth, easy, confident)

---

## Drill 4: Emotional Connection During Objection
Guest objection:
- “I’m not sure… it feels like a lot.”

Goal:
- name emotion (“That makes sense.”)
- validate
- simplify choices (2 options)
- micro-confirmation

---

## Drill 5: Peak-End Moment
Goal:
Create one “peak moment” + calm wrap-up:
- peak: “aha” benefit tied to their life
- end: confident next step

Debrief:
- What line felt the most human?
- What line felt like pressure?
