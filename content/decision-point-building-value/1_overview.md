## Objective
Guide guests to a confident decision by clarifying what matters most, translating features into real-life outcomes, and leading a calm, trust-first Decision Point conversation before numbers.

> “Clarity is kindness.”  
> — SignatureHyundai Standard

---

## What the Decision Point is
The Decision Point is the moment the guest quietly begins deciding:
- “This fits my life.”
- “I can see myself owning this.”
- “This might be the right vehicle.”

They may not say it out loud, but you can feel it.

<div class="quick-grid three">
  <div class="training-card">
    <span class="label-pill">Signal 1</span>
    <p>Questions shift from “What is it?” to “How would it work for me?”</p>
  </div>
  <div class="training-card">
    <span class="label-pill">Signal 2</span>
    <p>They start picturing real life, ownership, family, work, weather, payment, or timing.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="training-card">
    <span class="label-pill">Signal 3</span>
    <p>They compare, hesitate, or ask about price, payment, or next steps.</p>
  </div>
</div>

**Our job:** make the decision feel clear, safe, and simple.

---

## Why this module matters
Most deals are not lost on the vehicle.

They are lost when the guest feels:
- overwhelmed by options
- uncertain about fit
- afraid of regret
- unclear about the next step
- uncomfortable moving into numbers

<div class="do-dont">
  <div class="dont-box">
    <span class="label-pill">Weak Decision Point</span>
    <p>Jumping to numbers before fit is clear, pushing for commitment, or ignoring uncertainty.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="do-box">
    <span class="label-pill">Strong Decision Point</span>
    <p>Recap priorities, confirm fit, build value, ask a respectful micro-commitment, and transition to numbers as helpful information.</p>
  </div>
</div>
