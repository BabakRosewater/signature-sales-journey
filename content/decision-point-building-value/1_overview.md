# Module 5: Decision Point & Building Value

## Objective
Help guests reach a confident decision by clarifying what matters most, translating features into real-life outcomes, and using a calm, fact-based “Decision Point” conversation that keeps the process simple and trust-first.

> “People don’t want more information. They want more clarity.”
— SignatureHyundai Standard

---

## What the Decision Point Is
The Decision Point is the moment when the guest is close enough to say:
- “This fits my life,” and
- “I can see myself owning it,”

…but they still need:
- clarity,
- reassurance,
- and a simple next step.

This is where we **build value without pressure**.

---

## Why This Module Matters
Most deals aren’t lost because the vehicle is wrong.
They’re lost because:
- the guest feels overwhelmed,
- they can’t “connect the dots,”
- they’re afraid of regret,
- or the process feels unclear.

Our job is to reduce uncertainty and create confidence.

---

## What “Great” Looks Like
- You summarize what matters (in their words).
- You connect the vehicle to outcomes (not specs).
- You ask a calm commitment question (permission-based).
- You set up numbers as a next step—not a confrontation.

---

## The Decision Point Flow (Simple)
### 1) Clarify the “Why”
What matters most and why it matters.

### 2) Confirm the Fit
“This matches what you told me.”

### 3) Build Value (Outcomes)
Less stress. More confidence. Safer days. Easier routines.

### 4) Ask the Decision Point Question
A respectful micro-commitment that feels natural.

### 5) Transition to Next Step
“Want to see how the numbers look with the options you chose?”

---

## Success Outcome
By the end of this module, a guest should be able to say:
- “This makes sense.”
- “This feels like the right fit.”
- “Let’s see what it looks like.”
