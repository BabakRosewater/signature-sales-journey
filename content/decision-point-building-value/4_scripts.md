# 4.0 Scripts & Word Tracks

<div class="training-hero">
  <div class="eyebrow">Word Tracks • Decision Point</div>
  <div class="bigIdea">Use language that makes the next step feel clear, safe, and earned.</div>
  <p>These word tracks help salespeople recap priorities, build value, isolate uncertainty, and transition to numbers without pressure.</p>
</div>

<div class="do-dont">
  <div class="dont-box">
    <span class="label-pill">Avoid</span>
    <p>Jumping to numbers, pushing for commitment, ignoring hesitation, or using pressure language.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="do-box">
    <span class="label-pill">Use</span>
    <p>Guest’s words, value stack, fit checks, micro-commitments, and service-tone numbers transition.</p>
  </div>
</div>

---

## Decision Point setup
<div class="quick-grid two">
  <div class="training-card blue">
    <span class="label-pill">Hood Ornament Setup</span>
    <div class="script-line">“Let’s make sure this fits your life — not just looks good on paper.”</div>
  </div>

  <div class="training-card green">
    <span class="label-pill">15-Second Recap</span>
    <div class="script-line">“From what you told me, the big priorities are {P1}, {P2}, and {P3} — mostly because {WHY}.”</div>
  </div>
</div>

---

## Building value
<div class="quick-grid two">
  <div class="training-card amber">
    <span class="label-pill">Value Stack</span>
    <div class="script-line">“That’s why this one stands out: {fit reason}.”</div>
    <div class="script-line">“The part you’ll feel every day is {outcome}.”</div>
    <div class="script-line">“And it solves the concern you mentioned about {concern}.”</div>
  </div>

  <div class="training-card blue">
    <span class="label-pill">Feature → Outcome → Feeling</span>
    <div class="script-line">“Because you mentioned {need}, this {feature} helps give you {outcome/feeling}, especially when {real-life moment}.”</div>
  </div>

  <div class="training-card green">
    <span class="label-pill">Winter Confidence Example</span>
    <div class="script-line">“Because winter confidence matters, AWD helps you feel in control when the roads get messy.”</div>
  </div>

  <div class="training-card">
    <span class="label-pill">Family Safety Example</span>
    <div class="script-line">“Because family safety matters, this driver assist package gives you peace of mind on daily drives.”</div>
  </div>
</div>

---

## Decision Point questions
<div class="quick-grid three">
  <div class="training-card blue">
    <span class="label-pill">Fit Check</span>
    <div class="script-line">“On a scale of 1–10, how close is this to what you had in mind?”</div>
  </div>

  <div class="training-card green">
    <span class="label-pill">If-We-Could</span>
    <div class="script-line">“If we can make the numbers comfortable, would you feel good moving forward with this one?”</div>
  </div>

  <div class="training-card amber">
    <span class="label-pill">Two-Choice Clarity</span>
    <div class="script-line">“Which feels closer to your life — Option A or Option B?”</div>
  </div>
</div>

---

## Uncertainty and hesitation
<div class="quick-grid two">
  <div class="training-card amber">
    <span class="label-pill">I Need To Think</span>
    <div class="script-line">“Totally fair — this is a big decision. Before you think, what part feels unclear or uncertain right now?”</div>
    <div class="script-line">“Let’s make that part simple first.”</div>
  </div>

  <div class="training-card green">
    <span class="label-pill">Partner / Spouse Alignment</span>
    <div class="script-line">“Is there anything your partner will care about most — safety, payment, space, or something else — so we can make sure this checks that box too?”</div>
  </div>
</div>

---

## Transition to numbers
<div class="training-card dark">
  <span class="label-pill">Clean + Trust-First</span>
  <div class="script-line">“Would it be helpful if we looked at what this breaks down to with the options you chose — just so you can see it clearly?”</div>
</div>

---

## Manager coaching note
<div class="coach-check">
  <p><strong>Listen for this:</strong> The salesperson should recap the guest’s priorities before transitioning to numbers. If the guest cannot explain why the vehicle fits, the value conversation is not complete.</p>
</div>
