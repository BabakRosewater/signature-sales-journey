## Manager observation checklist
<div class="coach-check">
  <p><strong>During live observation, inspect:</strong></p>
  <ol>
    <li>Did the salesperson recap the guest’s priorities in the guest’s words?</li>
    <li>Did they explain why this vehicle fits?</li>
    <li>Did they build value through outcomes and feelings?</li>
    <li>Did they use a respectful micro-commitment?</li>
    <li>Did numbers feel like a helpful next step?</li>
  </ol>
  <p><strong>Result:</strong> Clear / Partially clear / Needs coaching</p>
</div>
