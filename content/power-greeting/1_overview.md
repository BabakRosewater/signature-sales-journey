## Objective
Establish a positive first impression that builds rapport, trust, and momentum.

> “Approach each customer with the idea of helping them solve a problem or achieve a goal — not of selling a product or service.”  
> — Brian Tracy

---

## Why this module matters
The first interaction anchors the guest’s perception of the salesperson, the dealership, and the rest of the visit.

A strong greeting tells the guest:
- we are organized
- we are professional
- we are here to help
- we are not going to pressure them
- they are safe to engage

<div class="do-dont">
  <div class="dont-box">
    <span class="label-pill">Avoid</span>
    <p>Rushing the guest, hovering, talking too fast, asking closed questions, or making the first moment feel like a sales pitch.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="do-box">
    <span class="label-pill">Use</span>
    <p>A warm welcome, calm posture, helpful question, and clear invitation to tell us what they want to accomplish.</p>
  </div>
</div>

## What this module builds
<div class="quick-grid three">
  <div class="training-card">

  </div>
