# 4.0 Role-Play Practice: Power Greetings

<div class="training-hero">
  <div class="eyebrow">Practice Reps</div>
  <div class="bigIdea">Make the first 10 seconds feel natural, calm, and helpful.</div>
  <p>Use these drills to practice tone, posture, timing, and first questions.</p>
</div>

<div class="score-row">
  <div class="score-card">1 = Rushed</div>
  <div class="score-card">3 = Clear</div>

## Scenario cards

<div class="quick-grid two">
  <div class="training-card blue">
    <span class="label-pill">Scenario 1 • Welcome to Showroom</span>
    <p><strong>Setup:</strong> Guest walks in and may be here for sales or service.</p>
    <p><strong>Goal:</strong> Confirm purpose and guide without pressure.</p>
    <div class="script-line">“Good afternoon — welcome in. What brings you in today?”</div>
    <div class="script-line">“Absolutely — I’ll walk you right over.”</div>
    <div class="script-line">“Perfect. I’m ____________. I’m here to help. What are you hoping to accomplish today?”</div>
    <p><strong>Coach notes:</strong> Confirm purpose first, remove pressure, give the guest control.</p>
  </div>

  <div class="training-card green">
    <span class="label-pill">Scenario 2 • Browser</span>
    <p><strong>Setup:</strong> Guest is walking the showroom or lot and appears to be browsing.</p>
    <p><strong>Goal:</strong> Acknowledge without hovering.</p>
    <div class="script-line">“Hi — has anyone greeted you yet?”</div>
    <div class="script-line">“Welcome. I’m ____________. Please make yourself at home.”</div>
    <div class="script-line">“Perfect — take your time. If you want features or pricing on any vehicle, I’ll help whenever you’re ready.”</div>
  </div>

  <div class="training-card amber">
    <span class="label-pill">Scenario 3 • Drive-Up Inquiry</span>
    <p><strong>Setup:</strong> Guest drives up and asks if we have a specific vehicle.</p>
    <p><strong>Goal:</strong> Help quickly and move into discovery naturally.</p>
    <div class="script-line">“Great question. Pull into customer parking and I’ll check that right now.”</div>
    <div class="script-line">“How long have you been considering one?”</div>
    <div class="script-line">“Great. I’m ____________. Is this your first visit with us?”</div>
  </div>

  <div class="training-card">
    <span class="label-pill">Scenario 4 • Service Guest</span>
    <p><strong>Setup:</strong> Guest is close to sales but appears to be here for service.</p>
    <p><strong>Goal:</strong> Be helpful first. Do not force a sales conversation.</p>
    <div class="script-line">“Are we getting you taken care of today?”</div>
    <div class="script-line">“Perfect. How has your __________ been treating you?”</div>
    <div class="script-line">“While you’re waiting, if you’re curious, I can show you what’s new. No pressure.”</div>
  </div>

  <div class="training-card blue">
    <span class="label-pill">Scenario 5 • Weather-Adapted Welcome</span>
    <p><strong>Setup:</strong> Guest arrives during cold, hot, wet, or uncomfortable weather.</p>
    <p><strong>Goal:</strong> Create comfort before sales conversation.</p>
    <div class="script-line">“Welcome — it’s [cold/hot/wet] out there. Let’s get you comfortable inside. Water or coffee?”</div>
    <div class="script-line">“What would you like to accomplish today — shopping or just starting research?”</div>
  </div>

  <div class="training-card red">
    <span class="label-pill">Scenario 6 • Busy Showroom</span>
    <p><strong>Setup:</strong> You are with another guest and a new guest enters.</p>
    <p><strong>Goal:</strong> Acknowledge quickly so no one feels ignored.</p>
    <div class="script-line">“Welcome in — I’ll be right with you.”</div>
    <p><strong>Coach notes:</strong> Eye contact, nod, warm tone, return quickly or ask a teammate to assist.</p>
  </div>
</div>

## Coaching debrief
<div class="quick-grid two">
  <div class="training-card">
    <span class="label-pill">Ask After Each Drill</span>
    <ol>
      <li>Did the greeting happen quickly?</li>
      <li>Did the salesperson sound calm and helpful?</li>
      <li>Did the guest feel welcomed instead of pressured?</li>
      <li>Was the first question open and useful?</li>
      <li>Was the next step clear?</li>
    </ol>
  </div>
  <div class="training-card amber">
    <span class="label-pill">Coach’s Watch Points</span>
    <ul>
      <li>Rushed tone</li>
      <li>Closed-off posture</li>
      <li>Talking while walking away</li>
      <li>Over-selling excitement</li>
      <li>Failure to acknowledge a waiting guest</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</div>
