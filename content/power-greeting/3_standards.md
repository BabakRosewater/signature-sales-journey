# 3.0 Greeting Standards

<div class="training-hero">
  <div class="eyebrow">Non-Negotiables</div>
  <div class="bigIdea">Warm, professional, helpful, and non-pushy.</div>
  <p>The greeting standard protects the guest experience before discovery begins.</p>
</div>

<div class="quick-grid four">
  <div class="training-card blue">
    <span class="label-pill">Timing</span>
    <p>Greet or acknowledge within <strong>10 seconds</strong>.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="training-card green">
    <span class="label-pill">Posture</span>
    <p>Open stance, shoulders back, calm pace, face the guest.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="training-card amber">
    <span class="label-pill">Tone</span>
    <p>Warm and professional, not overbearing or rushed.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="training-card dark">
    <span class="label-pill">Intent</span>
    <p>Make it feel like help, not pressure.</p>
  </div>
</div>

---

## Immediate engagement
<div class="quick-grid two">
  <div class="training-card">
    <span class="label-pill">If Available</span>
    <div class="script-line">“Welcome in. What brings you in today?”</div>
  </div>
  <div class="training-card amber">
    <span class="label-pill">If Busy</span>
    <div class="script-line">“Welcome in — I’ll be right with you.”</div>
    <p class="muted">Use eye contact, nod, and return quickly.</p>
  </div>
</div>

---

## Non-verbal communication checklist
<div class="quick-grid three">
  <div class="training-card"><span class="label-pill">Posture</span><p>Upright, shoulders back, no slouching.</p></div>
  <div class="training-card"><span class="label-pill">Stance</span><p>Open, feet grounded, not blocking the guest.</p></div>
  <div class="training-card"><span class="label-pill">Hands</span><p>Open palm gestures, no pointing.</p></div>
  <div class="training-card"><span class="label-pill">Eye Contact</span><p>Natural rhythm: connect, glance away, reconnect.</p></div>
  <div class="training-card"><span class="label-pill">Movement</span><p>Smooth, never abrupt or rushed.</p></div>
  <div class="training-card"><span class="label-pill">Mirroring</span><p>Match pace and tone subtly, without imitating.</p></div>
</div>

---

## Do / Don’t
<div class="do-dont">
  <div class="dont-box">
    <span class="label-pill">Avoid</span>
    <p>“Are you buying today?”</p>
    <p>“Can I help you?” when said in a way that invites “no.”</p>
    <p>Hovering, talking too fast, or starting with payment.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="do-box">
    <span class="label-pill">Use</span>
    <p>“Welcome in. What brings you in today?”</p>
    <p>“Are you looking for something specific, or just starting your search?”</p>
    <p>“I’m here to help — what are you hoping to accomplish today?”</p>
  </div>
</div>

## Scenario word tracks
<div class="quick-grid two">
  <div class="training-card blue">
    <span class="label-pill">Walk-In</span>
    <div class="script-line">“Hi — thanks for coming in. What brings you in today — shopping or just starting the research?”</div>
  </div>
  <div class="training-card green">
    <span class="label-pill">Drive-Up</span>
    <div class="script-line">“Welcome in. Is there something specific you came to see, or would you like a few options?”</div>
  </div>
  <div class="training-card amber">
    <span class="label-pill">Browser</span>
    <div class="script-line">“Has anyone had a chance to greet you yet?”</div>
    <div class="script-line">“Welcome — please take your time. If you want pricing or details, I’m right here whenever you’re ready.”</div>
  </div>
  <div class="training-card">
    <span class="label-pill">Service Guest</span>
    <div class="script-line">“Are we getting you taken care of today?”</div>
    <div class="script-line">“While you’re here, if you’re curious, I can show you what’s new — no pressure.”</div>
  </div>
  <div class="training-card blue">
    <span class="label-pill">Weather-Adapted</span>
    <div class="script-line">“It’s [cold/hot/wet] out there — let’s get you comfortable inside. Can I get you water or coffee while we look at options?”</div>
  </div>
  <div class="training-card green">
    <span class="label-pill">Known Guest</span>
    <div class="script-line">“Welcome back, Mr. Smith — good to see you.”</div>
  </div>
</div>

## Facility appearance standards
<div class="quick-grid three">
  <div class="training-card">
    <span class="label-pill">Entrance & Showroom</span>
    <p>Clean glass, floors, clear pathways, welcoming signage, uncluttered entry.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="training-card">
    <span class="label-pill">Workstations</span>
    <p>No food, old papers, or personal clutter visible to guests.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="training-card">
    <span class="label-pill">Vehicle Presentation</span>
    <p>Clean interiors, clean glass, no fingerprints, and clear information available.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="coach-check">
  <p><strong>Manager inspection question:</strong> Did the first 10 seconds create comfort, professionalism, and permission to continue?</p>
</div>
