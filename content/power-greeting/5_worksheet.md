## Manager observation checklist
<div class="coach-check">
  <p><strong>During live showroom observation, inspect:</strong></p>
  <ol>
    <li>Was the guest acknowledged within 10 seconds?</li>
    <li>Was posture open and professional?</li>
    <li>Was the tone warm and calm?</li>
    <li>Was the first question helpful and open?</li>
    <li>Did the guest appear comfortable moving forward?</li>
  </ol>
  <p><strong>Result:</strong> Clear / Partially clear / Needs coaching</p>
</div>
