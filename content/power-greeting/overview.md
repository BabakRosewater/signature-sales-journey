## Success Standards

- **10 seconds:** greet or acknowledge every guest (even if you’re busy)  
- **5 seconds:** professional image + open posture + warm tone  
- **Always:** make it feel like help, not pressure

---

## What you’ll practice in this module

- Facility standards that protect the brand

This module is built on proven principles: thin-slicing, halo effect, primacy, emotional contagion, expectation confirmation, servicescapes, and mirroring.

## Your greeting standards

We use a consistent greeting approach that is:
- **Warm**
- **Professional**
- **Helpful**
- **Non-pushy**
- **Adaptable to the moment**
