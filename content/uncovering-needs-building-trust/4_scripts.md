# 4.0 Scripts (Approved Talk Tracks)

## 4.1 Discovery Opening
“Welcome in—before we look at anything, I want to make sure we narrow this down fast.  
What brought you in today, and what’s most important in your next vehicle?”

## 4.2 Usage + Lifestyle
“Walk me through a normal week—commute, family, weather, trips.  
What does the vehicle need to *do*, not just *be*?”

## 4.3 Must-Haves / Deal-Breakers
“If we get everything else right, what are the 2–3 things you *must* have?”  
“And what’s a deal-breaker so we don’t waste your time?”

## 4.4 Budget Comfort Zone (Low Pressure)
“To keep this simple—do you have a payment comfort zone you’d like to stay near?”  
“Totally fine if you’re not sure—we can use a range.”

## 4.5 Early Appraisal Introduction
“If you have a trade, I’d like to start a quick appraisal now.  
It saves time and prevents surprises later—sound fair?”

## 4.6 Appraisal Transparency
“Our goal is accuracy and fairness. We’ll look at condition, history, and real market data.  
If you have questions as we go, I’ll walk through it with you.”

## 4.7 No Trade Transition
“Perfect—then we can focus fully on matching the right vehicle to your needs and budget.”

## 4.8 Service Customer Discovery
“How’s your vehicle been treating you? Still fitting your life, or have you been thinking about upgrading?”  
“While you’re here, would a quick trade appraisal be helpful just to know your options?”

## 4.9 Transition to Selection (Core)
“Let me recap to make sure I got it: you need (A), (B), and (C), and you’d like to stay around (budget/comfort).  
I have 2–3 options that fit—let’s look at them together.”
