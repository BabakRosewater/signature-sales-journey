# 5.0 Role-Play

## How to Run It
- Pair up (Customer / Consultant)
- 6 minutes per scenario
- Switch roles
- Score with the rubric

---

## Scenario 1: Family + Space + Safety
Customer needs a family vehicle, unsure on model. Concerned about safety and comfort.

**Goal:** uncover must-haves + timeline + budget comfort zone.

---

## Scenario 2: Towing + Daily Driving
Customer wants towing capability but worries about size and daily comfort.

**Goal:** clarify towing realities + usage patterns + present 2–3 right-fit directions.

---

## Scenario 3: Trade Distrust
Customer has a trade and expects the dealership to “lowball.”

**Goal:** early appraisal integration + transparency talk track + manager involvement if needed.

---

## Scenario 4: No Trade + Budget Focus
Customer sold privately. Wants best value and a clean process.

**Goal:** keep momentum; don’t “stall” because no trade exists.

---

## Role-Play Scorecard (0–2 each)
1) Open-ended questions first  
2) Active listening + summarizing  
3) Captured must-haves + deal-breakers  
4) Clarified budget/payment comfort  
5) Trade/no-trade handled correctly  
6) Transition to selection was confident + customer-focused

**Max Score:** 12
