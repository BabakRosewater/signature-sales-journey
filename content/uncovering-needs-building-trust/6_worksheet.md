## Manager observation checklist
<div class="coach-check">
  <p><strong>During live observation, inspect:</strong></p>
  <ol>
    <li>Did the salesperson ask open-ended questions first?</li>
    <li>Did they summarize the guest’s needs?</li>
    <li>Did they clarify budget or payment comfort without pressure?</li>
    <li>Did they introduce appraisal early when appropriate?</li>
    <li>Did they bridge to selection based on the guest’s priorities?</li>
  </ol>
  <p><strong>Result:</strong> Clear / Partially clear / Needs coaching</p>
</div>
