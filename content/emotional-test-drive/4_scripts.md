# 4.0 Scripts and Word Tracks (Copy/Paste Ready)

## 4.1 Pre-Drive Setup (60 Seconds)
“Before we head out, I want to make this easy. We’ll do a quick loop—about 10–12 minutes—so you can feel the ride, the visibility, and how it fits your day-to-day.
Before we move, what are the top two things you want to feel or confirm on this drive?”

---

## 4.2 Comfort + Control
“Are you comfortable with the seat and mirrors? Want the music on or quiet so you can hear the cabin?”

---

## 4.3 Values Bridge Sentence (Need → Feature → Feeling → Moment)
“Because you mentioned **{need}**, this **{feature}** helps give you **{feeling}**, especially when **{moment}**.”

Example:
“Because you mentioned winter driving, this AWD system helps give you confidence, especially when roads are slick.”

---

## 4.4 Future Pacing Prompt
“Picture your normal weekday. Where would this make life easier?”

---

## 4.5 Micro-Confirmation
“How does that feel compared to what you expected?”

---

## 4.6 The Peak Moment Setup (Highway Merge / Quiet Cabin / Tech Ease)
“Do you want to feel what it’s like merging? Nothing aggressive—just enough to see how smooth and confident it feels.”

---

## 4.7 If the Guest Seems Quiet or Unsure
“That’s totally okay—this is a lot to take in. What’s going through your mind right now?”

---

## 4.8 Post-Drive Close (Calm + Clear)
“Based on what you told me earlier—{priority 1} and {priority 2}—how close did this feel to what you had in mind?
What did you like most… and what would you change?”
