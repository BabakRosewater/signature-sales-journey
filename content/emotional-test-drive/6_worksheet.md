## Manager observation checklist
<div class="coach-check">
  <p><strong>During live observation, inspect:</strong></p>
  <ol>
    <li>Did the salesperson set up the route, time, and focus moment?</li>
    <li>Did they ask what the guest wanted to confirm?</li>
    <li>Did features connect to needs, feelings, and real-life moments?</li>
    <li>Did they use micro-confirmations?</li>
    <li>Did they ask post-drive fit questions?</li>
    <li>Was the next step clear?</li>
  </ol>
  <p><strong>Result:</strong> Clear / Partially clear / Needs coaching</p>
</div>
