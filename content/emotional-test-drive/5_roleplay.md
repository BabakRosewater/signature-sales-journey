# 5.0 Role-Play Lab (Manager-Led)

## Setup (Groups of 3)
- Consultant
- Guest
- Observer/Coach

Rotate every 8 minutes.

---

## Drill 1: 60-Second Pre-Drive Setup
Goal: hit all four:
- route/time
- top 2 priorities
- comfort/control
- focus moment

Observer scores:
- clarity (1–5)
- calm confidence (1–5)
- guest comfort (1–5)

---

## Drill 2: Values Bridge Reps (3 in a row)
Guest states:
- “Safety matters.”
- “I hate road noise.”
- “Winter driving worries me.”
- “I’m on the highway a lot.”

Goal: translate each into:
Need → Feature → Feeling → Moment

---

## Drill 3: Micro-Confirmations
Goal: ask 3 confirmations without sounding repetitive.
Examples must vary:
- feel check
- expectation check
- fit check

---

## Drill 4: Quiet Guest Mid-Drive
Guest becomes quiet and gives short answers.
Goal:
- name it calmly
- ask open question
- simplify to one focus moment

---

## Drill 5: Post-Drive Fit + Next Step
Goal:
- 3 fit questions
- one clear next step (compare option / numbers / alternate drive)
- no pressure language
