## Objective
Guide the guest through a test drive that connects features to feelings, reduces uncertainty, and builds confident ownership vision without pressure.

> “A sale is not something you pursue; it’s what happens to you while you are immersed in serving others.”  
> — Mike White

---

## Why this module matters
Most test drives fail for one simple reason:

<div class="do-dont">
  <div class="dont-box">
    <span class="label-pill">Weak Test Drive</span>
    <p>It becomes a demonstration, a route, or a feature tour.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="do-box">
    <span class="label-pill">Emotional Test Drive</span>
    <p>It becomes an experience where the guest can picture the vehicle fitting their life.</p>
  </div>
</div>

A great Emotional Test Drive does three things:

<div class="quick-grid three">
  <div class="training-card green">
    <span class="label-pill">Safety</span>
    <p>The guest feels comfortable, respected, and in control.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="training-card blue">
    <span class="label-pill">Meaning</span>
    <p>Features become benefits tied to the guest’s life.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="training-card amber">
    <span class="label-pill">Confidence</span>
    <p>The guest can imagine ownership and compare the vehicle honestly.</p>
  </div>
</div>

## Emotional Test Drive flow
<div class="flow-line">
  <span class="flow-step">Setup</span>
  <span class="flow-arrow">→</span>
  <span class="flow-step">Experience</span>
  <span class="flow-arrow">→</span>
  <span class="flow-step">Micro-confirm</span>
  <span class="flow-arrow">→</span>
  <span class="flow-step">Peak moment</span>
  <span class="flow-arrow">→</span>
  <span class="flow-step">Post-drive fit</span>
  <span class="flow-arrow">→</span>
  <span class="flow-step">Next step</span>
</div>

### Step 1 — Setup
Before the drive begins:
- set expectations
- explain route and time
- confirm top priorities
- choose 1–2 focus moments to highlight

### Step 2 — Experience
During the drive:
- prompt real-life visualization
- highlight only the features connected to guest priorities
- use micro-confirmations

### Step 3 — Confirm
After the drive:
- ask fit questions
- compare back to discovery
- clarify the next step without pressure
