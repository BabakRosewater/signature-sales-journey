# 5.0 Role-Play Drills

## 5.1 Full-Flow Drill
Run stages 1 through 11 without stopping. Focus on transitions and pace.

## 5.2 Objection Re-Entry Drill
Start at 4.10, resolve objection, then cleanly return to 4.11 close.

## 5.3 Delivery + Follow-Up Drill
Run stages 4.13 through 4.15 as one sequence focused on confidence and retention.

## 5.4 Scoring Focus
- Clarity of language
- Question quality
- Transition control
- Tone (calm, professional, no pressure)
- Close strength and next-step ownership
