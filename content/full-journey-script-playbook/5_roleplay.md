# 5.0 Role-Play Drills

<div class="training-hero">
  <div class="eyebrow">Playbook • Practice Reps</div>
  <div class="bigIdea">Practice the journey in sequences, not isolated scripts.</div>
  <p>The goal is to make transitions sound natural from greeting through follow-up.</p>
</div>

<div class="score-row">
  <div class="score-card">1 = Robotic</div>
  <div class="score-card">3 = Clear</div>
  <div class="score-card">5 = Natural + Useful</div>
</div>

---

## Drill cards

<div class="quick-grid two">
  <div class="training-card blue">
    <span class="label-pill">Drill 1 • Opening Sequence</span>
    <p><strong>Run:</strong> Greeting → Agenda → Discovery → Early Manager Support.</p>
    <p><strong>Focus:</strong> tone, pace, guest comfort, and clean stage transitions.</p>
    <p><strong>Coach checks:</strong> Did the manager support line feel natural?</p>
  </div>

  <div class="training-card green">
    <span class="label-pill">Drill 2 • Discovery to Selection</span>
    <p><strong>Run:</strong> Discovery close → Trade setup → Vehicle presentation.</p>
    <p><strong>Focus:</strong> connect the vehicle to what the guest said, not random features.</p>
  </div>

  <div class="training-card amber">
    <span class="label-pill">Drill 3 • Drive to Decision Point</span>
    <p><strong>Run:</strong> Emotional test drive → after-drive questions → hood ornament / Decision Point.</p>
    <p><strong>Focus:</strong> fit, value, comfort, and micro-commitment.</p>
  </div>

  <div class="training-card dark">
    <span class="label-pill">Drill 4 • Numbers to Close</span>
    <p><strong>Run:</strong> Tour → one-price setup → objection → close.</p>
    <p><strong>Focus:</strong> simple numbers, calm objection handling, and clear commitment ask.</p>
  </div>

  <div class="training-card red">
    <span class="label-pill">Drill 5 • Manager Close + Save-A-Deal</span>
    <p><strong>Run:</strong> manager handoff → diagnose issue → one ask → unsold follow-up plan.</p>
    <p><strong>Focus:</strong> manager lowers tension and protects the next step.</p>
  </div>

  <div class="training-card blue">
    <span class="label-pill">Drill 6 • Delivery + Follow-Up</span>
    <p><strong>Run:</strong> post-yes reassurance → delivery setup → same-day text → next-day call.</p>
    <p><strong>Focus:</strong> reduce buyer’s remorse, make ownership feel supported, and set the first service relationship.</p>
  </div>
</div>

## Scoring focus
<div class="coach-check">
  <p><strong>Score every rep on:</strong></p>
  <ol>
    <li>Clarity of language</li>
    <li>Question quality</li>
    <li>Transition control</li>
    <li>Calm, professional tone</li>
    <li>Guest-specific personalization</li>
    <li>Close strength and next-step ownership</li>
  </ol>
</div>

---

## Manager debrief questions
<div class="quick-grid two">
  <div class="training-card">
    <span class="label-pill">Ask After Each Drill</span>
    <ol>
      <li>Where did the language sound natural?</li>
      <li>Where did it sound scripted?</li>
      <li>Was the next stage clearly set up?</li>
      <li>Did the guest feel in control?</li>
      <li>What one line should we practice again?</li>
    </ol>
  </div>
  <div class="training-card amber">
    <span class="label-pill">Coach’s Watch Points</span>
    <ul>
      <li>Skipping manager support</li>
      <li>Skipping trade setup</li>
      <li>Feature dumping</li>
      <li>Jumping to numbers too early</li>
      <li>Unclear next step after objection</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</div>
