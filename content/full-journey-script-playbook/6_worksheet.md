# 6.0 Worksheet: Personal Script Builder + Practice Scorecard

## How to use this worksheet (2 minutes)
1) **Write it like you talk** (simple, natural language).  
2) Keep the **intent** of each stage (don’t change the purpose).  
3) Keep it **one breath** — most lines should be **1–2 sentences**.  
4) **Practice out loud**, then refine until it sounds like *you*.
