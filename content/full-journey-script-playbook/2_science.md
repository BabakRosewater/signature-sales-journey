## Core principles
<div class="quick-grid two">
  <div class="training-card blue">
    <span class="label-pill">Primacy Effect</span>
    <p>Early language shapes trust and expectations.</p>
    <p><strong>Use it:</strong> greeting and agenda scripts should make the guest feel welcomed, not pressured.</p>
  </div>

  <div class="training-card green">
    <span class="label-pill">Cognitive Load Reduction</span>
    <p>Simple structure reduces stress and indecision.</p>
    <p><strong>Use it:</strong> one stage, one intention, one clear next step.</p>
  </div>

  <div class="training-card amber">
    <span class="label-pill">Choice Architecture</span>
    <p>Two-option framing helps guests make decisions without feeling overwhelmed.</p>
    <p><strong>Use it:</strong> present clear paths, not endless scenarios.</p>
  </div>

  <div class="training-card">
    <span class="label-pill">Commitment + Consistency</span>
    <p>Confirming priorities increases forward movement because the next step connects to what the guest already said.</p>
    <p><strong>Use it:</strong> recap before selection, value, numbers, and close.</p>
  </div>

  <div class="training-card dark">
    <span class="label-pill">Peak-End Rule</span>
    <p>Delivery and follow-up shape long-term memory of the experience.</p>
    <p><strong>Use it:</strong> after-yes, delivery, and loyalty scripts must protect excitement and reduce confusion.</p>
  </div>

  <div class="training-card blue">
    <span class="label-pill">Manager Coaching</span>
    <p>Scripts create a coachable standard. Managers can hear whether the process is being followed.</p>
  </div>
</div>

## What script discipline is not
<div class="do-dont">
  <div class="dont-box">
    <span class="label-pill">Not This</span>
    <p>Robotic, fake, memorized, over-explained, or disconnected from what the guest said.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="do-box">
    <span class="label-pill">This</span>
    <p>A clear structure that helps the salesperson stay calm and the guest stay oriented.</p>
  </div>
</div>
