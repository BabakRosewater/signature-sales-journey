# 3.0 Success Standards

## 3.1 Communication Standards
- Lead with one clear intention per stage.
- Use short, plain sentences.
- Ask one decision-driving question before moving on.
- Confirm what the guest said before offering recommendations.

## 3.2 Process Standards
- Follow the stage order unless guest circumstances require adaptation.
- Transition explicitly between stages ("Next step is...").
- Never skip trade, test-drive, or numbers setup language.
- Always end with a defined next action.

## 3.3 Brand Standards
- Calm tone, factual language, no pressure.
- One-price clarity, no games.
- Respectful close, whether yes or no.
