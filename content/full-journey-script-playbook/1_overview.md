## Objective
Provide one complete, ready-to-use word-track library for every step of the sales journey — from first greeting through post-sale follow-up and Save-A-Deal recovery.

---

## How to use this playbook
<div class="quick-grid two">
  <div class="training-card blue">
    <span class="label-pill">For Salespeople</span>
    <ol>
      <li>Pick the stage you are currently in.</li>
      <li>Use the exact script first.</li>
      <li>Personalize only after the structure is natural.</li>
      <li>Stay concise and avoid over-explaining.</li>
    </ol>
  </div>
  <div class="training-card green">
    <span class="label-pill">For Managers</span>
    <ol>
      <li>Pick one stage for the sales meeting.</li>
      <li>Have each salesperson run one rep.</li>
      <li>Coach tone, timing, and transition.</li>
      <li>Inspect whether it sounds natural, clear, and guest-centered.</li>
    </ol>
  </div>
</div>
