# 4.0 Full Journey Scripts

<div class="training-hero">
  <div class="eyebrow">Playbook • Copyable Word Tracks</div>
  <div class="bigIdea">Use the right words at the right stage — then make them your own.</div>
  <p>Each card below is built for fast use in training, coaching, or live preparation. Copy the line, practice the structure, then personalize it with the guest’s real priorities.</p>
</div>

<div class="coach-check">
  <p><strong>Manager coaching note:</strong> Do not coach the whole playbook at once. Pick one stage, run reps, correct tone, and move to the next stage only after the language sounds natural.</p>
</div>

## 4.2 Set the Agenda
<div class="quick-grid two">
  <div class="training-card blue">
    <span class="label-pill">Reduce Anxiety</span>
    <div class="script-line">“Here’s how we do this so it’s easy: first, a few quick questions to understand what matters; second, we look at the right options; third, we take a drive; and if you love it, we review numbers. Sound fair?”</div>
  </div>
  <div class="training-card green">
    <span class="label-pill">One-Price Clarity</span>
    <div class="script-line">“Also, we’re one-price here. My job is to help you choose the right fit — not play games.”</div>
  </div>
</div>

## 4.3 Discovery
<div class="quick-grid two">
  <div class="training-card"><span class="label-pill">Why Now</span><div class="script-line">“What made you start looking — what’s changing?”</div></div>
  <div class="training-card"><span class="label-pill">Current Vehicle</span><div class="script-line">“What are you driving now, and what do you want to be better this time?”</div></div>
  <div class="training-card"><span class="label-pill">Priorities</span><div class="script-line">“Top three priorities: features, comfort, space, safety, warranty, color, style — what matters most?”</div></div>
  <div class="training-card"><span class="label-pill">Use Case</span><div class="script-line">“How do you use it weekly — commute, kids, road trips, snow, towing?”</div></div>
  <div class="training-card"><span class="label-pill">Budget Comfort</span><div class="script-line">“When you say ‘good payment,’ what range feels comfortable without stress?”</div></div>
  <div class="training-card"><span class="label-pill">Decision Makers</span><div class="script-line">“Besides you, who else needs to love this decision?”</div></div>
  <div class="training-card dark">
    <span class="label-pill">Close Discovery</span>
    <div class="script-line">“Okay — based on what you told me, the right direction is [Model/Trim A] and [Model/Trim B]. I’ll show both and we’ll let the drive make the decision.”</div>
  </div>
</div>

## 4.4 Early Manager Support
<div class="quick-grid two">
  <div class="training-card blue">
    <span class="label-pill">Set Expectation</span>
    <div class="script-line">“At some point early, I’ll introduce you to one of our managers. We do that so we can make sure we are building the right plan around what matters most to you — especially payment, trade, timing, and overall value.”</div>
  </div>
  <div class="training-card green">
    <span class="label-pill">Bring Manager In</span>
    <div class="script-line">“Before we go too far, I want to quickly introduce you to _____. We do this early so you know who is helping behind the scenes and so we keep everything aligned around what matters most to you.”</div>
  </div>
  <div class="training-card amber">
    <span class="label-pill">Manager Entry</span>
    <div class="script-line">“I appreciate you coming in. You’re in good hands with _____. Before we get too far, I like to ask one simple question: when you think about making this decision, what matters most — payment, trade, vehicle fit, timing, or overall value?”</div>
  </div>
</div>

## 4.6 Vehicle Presentation
<div class="quick-grid two">
  <div class="training-card blue"><span class="label-pill">Connect First</span><div class="script-line">“Before I show you anything, let me connect what you told me to the vehicle.”</div></div>
  <div class="training-card green"><span class="label-pill">Need → Benefit</span><div class="script-line">“You said [priority] matters most — this one is strong there because [benefit].”</div></div>
  <div class="training-card amber"><span class="label-pill">Two Things</span><div class="script-line">“Two things I want you to notice right away: [benefit 1] and [benefit 2].”</div></div>
  <div class="training-card"><span class="label-pill">Multiple Vehicles</span><div class="script-line">“We’re going to do this the smart way: I’ll show you the difference that actually matters, not 40 random features.”</div></div>
</div>

## 4.7 Emotional Test Drive
<div class="quick-grid two">
  <div class="training-card blue"><span class="label-pill">Before Drive</span><div class="script-line">“On this drive, I want you to test it like it’s already yours: braking, merging, turning radius, visibility, and how quiet it is.”</div></div>
  <div class="training-card green"><span class="label-pill">Winning Standard</span><div class="script-line">“What would make you say, ‘Yep — this is the one’?”</div></div>
  <div class="training-card"><span class="label-pill">Seating</span><div class="script-line">“How does the seating feel — more relaxed than your current one?”</div></div>
  <div class="training-card"><span class="label-pill">Confidence</span><div class="script-line">“When you accelerate, does it feel confident enough for your daily driving?”</div></div>
  <div class="training-card amber"><span class="label-pill">1–10 Fit</span><div class="script-line">“On a scale of 1–10, how close is this to what you want?”</div></div>
  <div class="training-card dark"><span class="label-pill">After Drive</span><div class="script-line">“What did you like most?”</div><div class="script-line">“Anything you’d change if you could?”</div><div class="script-line">“If we can make the numbers work comfortably, do you feel good moving forward with this exact vehicle?”</div></div>
</div>

## 4.9 Decision Point / Hood Ornament Moment
<div class="quick-grid two">
  <div class="training-card blue"><span class="label-pill">Between Options</span><div class="script-line">“Between the two options, which one fits you better — and why?”</div></div>
  <div class="training-card green"><span class="label-pill">Future Ownership</span><div class="script-line">“If you woke up tomorrow and both were in your driveway, which keys would you grab?”</div></div>
  <div class="training-card dark"><span class="label-pill">Commit to Unit</span><div class="script-line">“Great — so we’re choosing this one: [year/model/trim/color]. If we keep everything simple and the numbers are comfortable, are you ready to move forward?”</div></div>
</div>

## 4.10 Numbers / One-Price Presentation
<div class="quick-grid two">
  <div class="training-card blue"><span class="label-pill">Full Picture</span><div class="script-line">“Here’s the full picture. This is our best price on the vehicle. From there it’s just math: trade, taxes, and how you want to structure the payment.”</div></div>
  <div class="training-card green"><span class="label-pill">Finance vs Lease</span><div class="script-line">“Most guests choose one of two paths: finance for ownership, or lease for flexibility. Which direction matches your goals?”</div></div>
  <div class="training-card amber"><span class="label-pill">Payment Pressure</span><div class="script-line">“Totally fair. Payment is just a result of three things: price, rate, and term. We’ll adjust the structure — not the truth.”</div></div>
</div>

## 4.11 Objection Handling
<div class="quick-grid two">
  <div class="training-card amber"><span class="label-pill">Need To Think</span><div class="script-line">“Of course. When people say that, it’s usually one of three things: the vehicle, the numbers, or the timing. Which one is it for you?”</div></div>
  <div class="training-card blue"><span class="label-pill">Shopping Dealers</span><div class="script-line">“You should. The question is: what do you want to compare — the vehicle, or the experience? Because price here is straightforward — and we’ll make the ownership side easy too.”</div></div>
  <div class="training-card green"><span class="label-pill">Sleep On It</span><div class="script-line">“Totally fair. Before you go, what would need to be true tomorrow for you to feel confident saying yes?”</div></div>
