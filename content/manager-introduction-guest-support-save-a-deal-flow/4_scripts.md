# 4.0 Scripts & Word Tracks

<div class="training-hero">
  <div class="eyebrow">Word Tracks • Use Today</div>
  <div class="bigIdea">Make the manager introduction sound natural, mature, and guest-centered.</div>
  <p>The goal is not to “bring in a closer.” The goal is to normalize support and build the right plan around what matters most to the guest.</p>
</div>

<div class="do-dont">
  <div class="dont-box">
    <span class="label-pill">Avoid</span>
    <p>“Let me get my manager.”</p>
    <p>“My manager wants to make sure we are good listeners.”</p>
    <p>“I need to check with my manager.”</p>
  </div>
  <div class="do-box">
    <span class="label-pill">Use</span>
    <p>“I want to introduce you to one of our managers early.”</p>
    <p>“We do this so we can build the right plan around what matters most to you.”</p>
    <p>“Our goal is to keep this simple and help you make a confident decision.”</p>
  </div>
</div>

## Salesperson word tracks

<div class="quick-grid two">
  <div class="training-card blue">
    <span class="label-pill">Early Setup</span>
    <p class="muted">Use after initial discovery, before vehicle presentation or numbers.</p>
    <div class="script-line">“Just so you know how we do things here, I’ll help you through the vehicle side, and I’ll introduce you to one of our managers early in the process. We do that because affordability means something different to every guest right now.”</div>
    <div class="script-line">“Our goal is to make sure we understand what matters most to you — payment, trade, timing, vehicle fit, and overall value — so we can build the right plan around you.”</div>
  </div>

  <div class="training-card green">
    <span class="label-pill">Short Daily Version</span>
    <p class="muted">Use when you need one simple, repeatable line.</p>
    <div class="script-line">“At some point early, I’ll introduce you to one of our managers. We do that so we can make sure we are building the right plan around what matters most to you — especially payment, trade, timing, and overall value.”</div>
  </div>

  <div class="training-card">
    <span class="label-pill">First Manager Introduction</span>
    <p class="muted">Use when bringing the manager in after discovery.</p>
    <div class="script-line">“Before we go too far, I want to quickly introduce you to _____. He’s one of our sales managers. We do this early so you know who is helping behind the scenes and so we can keep everything aligned around what matters most to you.”</div>
  </div>

  <div class="training-card amber">
    <span class="label-pill">Before Numbers</span>
    <p class="muted">Use before presenting structure or payment options.</p>
    <div class="script-line">“Before we put everything together, I want to bring _____ back in for a minute. That way we can make sure the structure matches what you told me matters most.”</div>
  </div>
</div>

## Manager word tracks

<div class="quick-grid two">
  <div class="training-card dark">
    <span class="label-pill">Manager Entry</span>
    <p class="muted">Enter with context. Support the salesperson. Ask one useful question.</p>
    <div class="script-line">“I appreciate you coming in. You are in good hands with _____. Before we get too far, I like to ask one simple question: when you think about making this decision, what matters most — payment, trade, vehicle fit, timing, or overall value?”</div>
  </div>

  <div class="training-card blue">
    <span class="label-pill">Before Numbers</span>
    <p class="muted">Confirm the target before structure is presented.</p>
    <div class="script-line">“I want to make sure we build this around the right target. Is the biggest focus payment, trade value, money down, timing, or making sure this is the right vehicle?”</div>
  </div>

  <div class="training-card amber">
    <span class="label-pill">Guest Is Drifting</span>
    <p class="muted">Use when the guest is losing energy or becoming unclear.</p>
    <div class="script-line">“What part feels unclear or not comfortable yet?”</div>
  </div>

  <div class="training-card green">
    <span class="label-pill">Need To Think</span>
    <p class="muted">Respect the guest while clarifying the real issue.</p>
    <div class="script-line">“Totally fair. When people say that, it is usually one of a few things: the vehicle, the numbers, the trade, the timing, or just needing more confidence. Which one is it for you?”</div>
    <div class="script-line">“If we solve that part, are we still in the right conversation?”</div>
  </div>
</div>

## Common pressure points

<div class="quick-grid two">
  <div class="training-card">
    <span class="label-pill">Guest Asks Why</span>
    <p class="muted">When the guest asks why they need to meet a manager.</p>
    <div class="script-line">“Fair question. You don’t need to do anything extra. We introduce our managers early so you know who is helping support the process before there is ever a question. It keeps everything simple.”</div>
    <div class="script-line">“My goal is not to slow you down. I just want to make sure we understand the right target before we start putting options together.”</div>
  </div>

  <div class="training-card red">
    <span class="label-pill">Before Guest Leaves</span>
    <p class="muted">Use this before the opportunity leaves without clarity.</p>
    <div class="script-line">“Before you head out, I want to have _____ say goodbye and make sure we understand the best next step. That way we do not just send you generic follow-up.”</div>
    <div class="script-line">“I do not want to pressure you. I just want to make sure we understand what would need to be different for this to make sense.”</div>
  </div>
</div>

## Same-day follow-up

<div class="quick-grid two">
  <div class="training-card blue">
    <span class="label-pill">Manager Text</span>
    <div class="script-line">“Hi [Name], this is [Manager] at Clark Hyundai. I appreciated you coming in today. I know the main question was [payment/trade/timing/vehicle fit]. I am going to work with [Salesperson] on the cleanest next option and follow up with you by [time].”</div>
  </div>

  <div class="training-card green">
    <span class="label-pill">Salesperson Text</span>
    <div class="script-line">“Hi [Name], this is [Salesperson]. Thank you again for spending time with us today. I spoke with [Manager] after you left, and we are working on the part you said mattered most: [specific concern]. I will follow up at [time] with the next best option.”</div>
  </div>
</div>
