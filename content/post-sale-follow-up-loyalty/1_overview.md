## Objective
Turn delivery into long-term loyalty by executing a consistent post-sale follow-up system that protects trust, builds confidence in ownership, and creates repeat/referral momentum without sounding scripted or pushy.

> “The sale is not the end of the relationship. It is the beginning of the reputation.”  
> — SignatureHyundai Standard

---

## Why this module matters
Most customers do not leave a dealership because of one big mistake.

They leave because:
- nobody followed up
- service felt unfamiliar
- problems felt ignored
- they did not feel remembered
- ownership felt harder than expected

<div class="do-dont">
  <div class="dont-box">
    <span class="label-pill">Weak Follow-Up</span>
    <p>Generic “just checking in,” no service introduction, no problem ownership, or only calling when you need something.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="do-box">
    <span class="label-pill">Strong Follow-Up</span>
    <p>Helpful cadence, personalized notes, ownership support, service confidence, and earned review/referral asks.</p>
  </div>
</div>
