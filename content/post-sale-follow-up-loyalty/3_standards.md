# 3.0 Success Standards (Non-Negotiables)

## Standard 1: Follow-Up Cadence (Minimum)
- 24–48 hours after delivery
- 7 days after delivery
- 30 days after delivery
- 90 days after delivery (ownership check + loyalty touch)
- Service milestone touches (first service reminder + check-in)

---

## Standard 2: Follow-Up Purpose = Help, Not “Checking In”
Every contact must have a reason:
- answer questions
- help with features
- confirm satisfaction
- introduce service support
- handle anything unresolved

Avoid empty: “Just checking in.”

---

## Standard 3: Use Notes to Personalize
Reference something real:
- their priorities
- family/lifestyle
- trade story
- commute
- weekend plans
- what they were excited about

---

## Standard 4: One Ask Per Touch (Max)
Don’t stack asks (review + referral + service + accessories).
Choose one, based on timing and value delivered.

---

## Standard 5: If There’s a Problem, Own It and Solve It
No blame. No passing.
- acknowledge
- take responsibility for the next step
- update them until resolved

---

## Standard 6: Service Intro Must Be Warm
Service should feel like:
- a benefit
- a support team
- a relationship

Never: “Go see service.”

---

## Standard 7: CRM Discipline
Every follow-up must be logged with:
- outcome
- next action
- any open loops
- service appointment status
