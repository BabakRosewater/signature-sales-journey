# 4.0 Scripts and Word Tracks

## 4.1 24–48 Hour Follow-Up (Help First)
“Hey {Name}, it’s {Your Name} from SignatureHyundai.  
Quick one — I wanted to make sure your first day or two with the {Vehicle} has been smooth.

What’s one thing you love so far… and is there anything that feels confusing or annoying that I can help with?”

---

## 4.2 7-Day Follow-Up (Ownership Confidence)
“Now that you’ve had a full week, how’s it fitting your day-to-day?  
Anything you’d like me to walk you through — settings, phone, safety tech, or anything else?”

---

## 4.3 30-Day Follow-Up (Service Intro)
“You’re about a month in now — how are things going overall?  
Also, I want to make your first service visit easy. Would you like me to help you get your first appointment set up and tell you exactly what to expect?”

---

## 4.4 Problem Handling (Own + Solve)
“Thank you for telling me. That makes sense, and I’m going to help you get this handled.  
Here’s what I’m going to do next: {next step}.  
I’ll update you by {time/day} so you’re not left guessing.”

---

## 4.5 Review Ask (Earned, Simple)
“If you feel like we took good care of you, would you be open to leaving a quick review?  
It helps our team a lot, and I’d really appreciate it.”

---

## 4.6 Referral Ask (Natural)
“If anyone in your circle mentions they’re thinking about a vehicle, feel free to send them my way.  
I’ll take care of them the same way.”

---

## 4.7 90-Day Loyalty Touch (Human)
“Hey {Name} — quick hello. How’s the {Vehicle} treating you now that you’ve had some time with it?  
Anything you want to improve about the ownership experience?”

---

## 4.8 “Not Pushy” Re-Engage (If They Mention Someone Shopping)
“No pressure at all — if they want options, I can make it simple and send a couple best-fit ideas.”
