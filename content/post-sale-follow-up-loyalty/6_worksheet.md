## Manager observation checklist
<div class="coach-check">
  <p><strong>During follow-up inspection, check:</strong></p>
  <ol>
    <li>Was the follow-up completed on time?</li>
    <li>Did the message include a helpful purpose?</li>
    <li>Was it personalized with real notes?</li>
    <li>Was there only one ask?</li>
    <li>Was the outcome and next action logged in CRM?</li>
  </ol>
  <p><strong>Result:</strong> Clear / Partially clear / Needs coaching</p>
</div>
