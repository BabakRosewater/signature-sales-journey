# 5.0 Role-Play Lab (Manager-Led)

## Setup (Groups of 3)
- Consultant
- Guest
- Observer/Coach

Rotate every 8 minutes.

---

## Drill 1: 24–48 Hour Help Call
Goal:
- warm tone
- ask “love + friction”
- offer one specific help option

Guest response options:
- “I love it.”
- “I’m confused by the tech.”
- “I’m annoyed by a setting.”

Observer scores:
- Human tone (1–5)
- Helpful framing (1–5)
- Clear next step (1–5)

---

## Drill 2: 30-Day Service Intro
Goal:
- frame service as support
- ask permission to schedule
- explain what to expect

Guest objection:
- “I’ll do it later.”
- “I don’t like service departments.”

---

## Drill 3: Problem Ownership
Guest says:
- “Nobody called me back about ___."
Goal:
- acknowledge
- own next step
- promise update time
- follow-through language

---

## Drill 4: Review Ask (Earned)
Goal:
- ask after value is delivered
- keep it short
- no pressure tone

---

## Drill 5: Referral Ask (Natural)
Goal:
- make it casual and respectful
- do not “pitch”
- connect to trust
