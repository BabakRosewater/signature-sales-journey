## Objective
Use a short, purposeful dealership tour to increase comfort, credibility, and trust — introducing key people and touchpoints that reinforce how we support the guest before, during, and after the sale.

> “Trust isn’t built by what we say. It’s built by what we prove.”  
> — SignatureHyundai Standard

---

## Why we do the tour
A great tour is not about showing off the building.

It shows the guest:
- where support comes from
- who is on their team
- what ownership will feel like here
- how service and follow-up work after the sale

<div class="do-dont">
  <div class="dont-box">
    <span class="label-pill">Weak Tour</span>
    <p>Long, awkward, scripted, corny, or disconnected from the guest’s decision.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="do-box">
    <span class="label-pill">Strong Tour</span>
    <p>Short, purposeful, helpful, and focused on support after the sale.</p>
  </div>
</div>

---

## When to do it
Best timing is after the guest has a strong fit feeling:
- the emotional test drive is landing
- the Decision Point is forming
- the guest is leaning positive
- the next step is becoming numbers or commitment

If you do it too early, it feels like a script.

If you do it too late, it feels like a stall.

## The tour framework
<div class="flow-line">
  <span class="flow-step">Permission</span>
  <span class="flow-arrow">→</span>
  <span class="flow-step">Purpose</span>
  <span class="flow-arrow">→</span>
  <span class="flow-step">Service proof</span>
  <span class="flow-arrow">→</span>
  <span class="flow-step">Key introduction</span>
  <span class="flow-arrow">→</span>
  <span class="flow-step">Comfort touchpoint</span>
  <span class="flow-arrow">→</span>
  <span class="flow-step">Next step</span>
</div>

<div class="quick-grid two">
  <div class="training-card blue">
    <span class="label-pill">1 • Permission</span>
    <div class="script-line">“This will take about 3 minutes — can I show you something quick that makes ownership here easier?”</div>
  </div>
  <div class="training-card green">
    <span class="label-pill">2 • Purpose</span>
    <div class="script-line">“I want you to see how we support you after the sale — so it’s not just talk.”</div>
  </div>
  <div class="training-card amber">
    <span class="label-pill">3 • Service Proof</span>
    <p>Show service drive, advisor check-in, or scheduling support.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="training-card">
    <span class="label-pill">4 • Key Introductions</span>
    <p>Manager, service advisor, business office, or delivery specialist — only if they add confidence.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="training-card dark">
