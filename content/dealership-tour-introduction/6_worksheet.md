## Manager observation checklist
<div class="coach-check">
  <p><strong>During live observation, inspect:</strong></p>
  <ol>
    <li>Did the salesperson ask permission or explain purpose?</li>
    <li>Was the tour 3–6 minutes?</li>
    <li>Was there one service proof moment?</li>
    <li>Were introductions useful and not excessive?</li>
    <li>Did the tour transition clearly to the next step?</li>
  </ol>
  <p><strong>Result:</strong> Clear / Partially clear / Needs coaching</p>
</div>
