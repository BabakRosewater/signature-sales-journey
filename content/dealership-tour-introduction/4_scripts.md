# 4.0 Scripts & Word Tracks

## 4.1 Tour Permission (Simple)
“This will take about 3 minutes—can I show you something quick that makes ownership here easier?”

---

## 4.2 Tour Purpose
“I want you to see how we support you after the sale—so it’s not just talk.”

---

## 4.3 Service Credibility Line
“This is our service drive. This is where you’ll check in, and we keep scheduling simple.”

---

## 4.4 Service Advisor Intro (Quick)
“Hey {Name}, this is {Guest}. They’re looking at {Model}. I just wanted you two to meet—this is who helps you once you own it.”

---

## 4.5 Comfort Touchpoint
“Waiting area is right here—coffee, Wi-Fi, and restrooms are just around the corner.”

---

## 4.6 If Guest Seems Rushed
“No worries—let’s keep this super quick. I just want you to know exactly where support is.”

---

## 4.7 Transition to Numbers (Clean)
“Now that you’ve seen how we support you, let’s sit down and look at the numbers clearly.”

---

## 4.8 If Guest Has Service Anxiety
“Totally fair. Service is where trust is earned long-term. That’s why I wanted you to see it—so you know what it’s like here.”
