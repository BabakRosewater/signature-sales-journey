# 5.0 Role-Play Lab (Manager-Led)

## Setup (Groups of 3)
- Consultant
- Guest
- Observer/Coach
Rotate every 6–7 minutes.

---

## Drill 1: 60-Second Purpose + Permission
Goal:
- permission line
- purpose line
- calm tone

Guest prompt:
“Why are we walking around?”

---

## Drill 2: Service Proof Moment
Goal:
- explain service in 2 sentences
- 1 clean introduction (optional)
- no rambling

Observer scores:
- clarity (1–5)
- confidence (1–5)
- not awkward (1–5)

---

## Drill 3: Rushed Guest
Guest prompt:
“I’m short on time.”

Goal:
- validate
- compress tour
- transition cleanly

---

## Drill 4: Tour → Numbers Transition
Goal:
Make numbers feel like the natural next step.
No pressure energy.

---

## Drill 5: Guest Concern About Service
Guest prompt:
“I’ve had bad service experiences.”

Goal:
- validate
- connect tour to support story
- invite next step
