# 4.0 Scripts & Word Tracks

## 4.1 Transition Into Numbers (after tour)
“Now that you’ve seen how we support you, I’ll keep the numbers simple and clear. Sound good?”

---

## 4.2 30-Second Recap (Values)
“From what you told me, the big priorities are: **{Priority 1}**, **{Priority 2}**, and **{Priority 3}**—especially because {why}. Did I get that right?”

---

## 4.3 Numbers Setup (reduce anxiety)
“I’m going to show you one best-fit option first. If we need to adjust, we’ll do it together.”

---

## 4.4 Confirm Alignment
“How close is this to what you had in mind?”

---

## 4.5 The Commitment Ask (simple)
“Would you like to move forward with this one?”

---

## 4.6 Two-Option Close (choice comfort)
“Totally fair. We have two clean paths:
- **Option A:** {lower payment / longer term}
- **Option B:** {shorter term / faster equity}
Which fits your life better?”

---

## 4.7 ‘I Need to Think About It’
“Of course. Before you go—what specifically would you like to think through?
Is it the vehicle, the payment, or just timing?”

(Then isolate and solve.)
“If we could make that one piece feel comfortable, would you be ready to move forward?”

---

## 4.8 ‘I Want to Shop Around’
“That makes sense—this is a big decision.
Most people compare two things: **fit** and **numbers**.
Is there anything about the fit that feels uncertain, or is it mainly the numbers?”

(If numbers:)
“Let’s make sure you’re comparing apples to apples. What would you like to use as your ‘must-have’ baseline?”

---

## 4.9 Payment Too High (calm)
“I hear you. Let’s solve it cleanly.
Is it the monthly number itself, or how it fits your budget priorities?”

(Then offer two paths:)
- adjust term/down/payment/vehicle
- or alternate selection if needed

---

## 4.10 ‘I Need to Talk to {Spouse/Partner}’
“Absolutely—totally normal.
What’s the most important part for them—payment, safety, or overall value?
If you’d like, we can send a simple summary so it’s easy to review together.”
