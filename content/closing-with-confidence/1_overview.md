## Objective
Guide the guest to a clear, confident decision by presenting numbers simply, confirming alignment with their priorities, addressing concerns calmly, and asking for commitment without pressure.

> “Clarity is kindness.”  
> — SignatureHyundai Standard

---

## Why this module matters
Most deals do not die because the vehicle was wrong.

They die because:
- the numbers felt confusing
- the guest felt pressured
- the next step was not clear
- fear of regret took over
- the salesperson filled silence or over-explained

<div class="do-dont">
  <div class="dont-box">
    <span class="label-pill">Weak Close</span>
    <p>Over-talking, bouncing between options, pressure language, unclear commitment ask, or vague follow-up.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="do-box">
    <span class="label-pill">Confident Close</span>
    <p>Calm recap, simple numbers, strategic silence, alignment check, respectful commitment ask, and clear next steps.</p>
  </div>
</div>
