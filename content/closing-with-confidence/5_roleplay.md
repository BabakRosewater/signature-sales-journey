# 5.0 Role-Play Lab (Manager-Led)

## Setup (Groups of 3)
- Consultant
- Guest
- Observer/Coach
Rotate every 7–8 minutes.

---

## Drill 1: Recap → Numbers → Confirm (2 minutes)
Goal:
- 30-second recap
- numbers setup line
- confirm alignment

Guest prompt:
“Okay… so what are the numbers?”

---

## Drill 2: Strategic Silence
Goal:
Present numbers in 20–30 seconds, then pause.
Observer checks:
- did the consultant fill silence?
- did the guest speak first?

---

## Drill 3: Two-Option Close
Guest prompt:
“I’m not sure.”

Goal:
- validate
- offer 2 options
- ask preference

---

## Drill 4: ‘I Need to Think’
Goal:
- isolate root concern
- solve one piece
- re-check readiness

---

## Drill 5: Payment Objection
Guest prompt:
“That payment is too high.”

Goal:
- clarify: number vs budget fit
- propose 2 paths
- confirm alignment again

---

## Drill 6: Clean Next Steps (No Sale Today)
Goal:
End with trust intact:
- summarize
- next step
- timeframe
- promise of follow-up
