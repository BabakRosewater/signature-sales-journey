# 3.0 Success Standards (Non-Negotiables)

## Standard 1: Recap Before Numbers
Always summarize what matters most to them first.

---

## Standard 2: One Page, One Direction
Don’t bounce between scenarios endlessly.
Start with the best-fit path.

---

## Standard 3: Calm Pace + Strategic Silence
Present. Pause. Let them think.

---

## Standard 4: Confirm Before You Close
Before commitment ask, confirm alignment:
“Is this matching what you had in mind?”

---

## Standard 5: Two-Option Close (When Needed)
If uncertain, offer two clear options—not five.

---

## Standard 6: Separate Question vs Concern vs Objection
- Question: clarify info
- Concern: validate + reassure
- Objection: isolate + solve + re-check

---

## Standard 7: No Pressure Language
Avoid:
- “If you do it today…”
- “What do I have to do to earn your business?”
- guilt / fear tactics

We build urgency through clarity and truth only.

---

## Standard 8: Clean Next Steps Always
Even if they don’t buy today:
- summarize
- set a next step
- keep trust intact
