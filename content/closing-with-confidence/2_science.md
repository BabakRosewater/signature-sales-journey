## Six closing principles

<div class="quick-grid two">
  <div class="training-card blue">
    <span class="label-pill">1 • Cognitive Load</span>
    <p>When information feels heavy, people delay.</p>
    <p><strong>Use it:</strong> keep numbers simple, summarize first, pause and let the guest process.</p>
    <div class="script-line">“I’ll keep this simple.”</div>
  </div>

  <div class="training-card amber">
    <span class="label-pill">2 • Loss Aversion</span>
    <p>Guests often feel the risk of making a mistake more than the joy of getting a great car.</p>
    <p><strong>Use it:</strong> emphasize protection, fit, and confidence without pressure.</p>
  </div>

  <div class="training-card green">
    <span class="label-pill">3 • Choice Architecture</span>
    <p>Two clear options reduce anxiety better than five possible directions.</p>
    <div class="script-line">“Option A protects payment. Option B builds equity faster. Which fits your life better?”</div>
  </div>

  <div class="training-card">
    <span class="label-pill">4 • Commitment + Consistency</span>
    <p>When guests affirm what matters to them, they are more likely to act consistently with it.</p>
    <p><strong>Use it:</strong> recap priorities and tie numbers back to those priorities.</p>
  </div>

  <div class="training-card blue">
    <span class="label-pill">5 • Procedural Fairness</span>
    <p>People accept outcomes more when the process feels fair, transparent, and respectful.</p>
    <div class="script-line">“I’ll show you the path clearly, and if we need to adjust, we’ll do it together.”</div>
  </div>

  <div class="training-card dark">
    <span class="label-pill">6 • Peak-End</span>
    <p>Guests remember the emotional ending.</p>
    <p><strong>Use it:</strong> end with a calm next step and confirm the guest feels in control.</p>
  </div>
</div>

## What happens if we over-talk?
<div class="do-dont">
  <div class="dont-box">
    <span class="label-pill">Over-Talking</span>
    <p>Salesperson fills silence, adds too many scenarios, and makes the guest more uncertain.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="do-box">
    <span class="label-pill">Strategic Silence</span>
    <p>Salesperson presents clearly, pauses, and lets the guest process and respond first.</p>
  </div>
</div>
