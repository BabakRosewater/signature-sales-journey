## Manager observation checklist
<div class="coach-check">
  <p><strong>During live observation, inspect:</strong></p>
  <ol>
    <li>Did the salesperson recap before numbers?</li>
    <li>Were numbers simple and easy to understand?</li>
    <li>Did the salesperson pause and let the guest process?</li>
    <li>Did they confirm alignment before asking for commitment?</li>
    <li>If not sold, was the next step clear?</li>
  </ol>
  <p><strong>Result:</strong> Clear / Partially clear / Needs coaching</p>
</div>
