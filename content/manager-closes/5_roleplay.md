# 5.0 Role-Play Lab (Manager + Consultant)

## Setup (Groups of 3)
- Sales Consultant
- Manager
- Guest

Rotate every 7–8 minutes.

---

## Drill 1: Clean Handoff (30 seconds)
Goal: Consultant sets up the manager turn without “uh-oh energy.”
Score:
- Confidence (1–5)
- Guest comfort (1–5)

---

## Drill 2: Diagnose the Category
Guest prompt examples:
- “I just need to think.” (misalignment or concern)
- “I saw one online cheaper.” (objection)
- “I’m not ready to commit.” (uncertainty)
- “I don’t understand the numbers.” (question)

Goal:
- Ask the diagnose question
- Label the category correctly

---

## Drill 3: Unshakable Facts Summary
Goal: deliver 3 facts calmly, then one recommended path.
No debate. No pressure.

---

## Drill 4: Two Options + One Ask
Goal:
- Offer two options max
- End with one clean ask
Time limit: 90 seconds

---

## Drill 5: Consultant Stays the Hero
Goal:
- Manager supports consultant
- Manager hands the guest back cleanly:
“_____ will take great care of the next step.”
