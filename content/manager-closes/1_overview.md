## Objective
Use a structured, low-pressure manager turn to clarify truth, resolve concerns, and align next steps so the guest feels supported and the deal stays clean.

> “The manager close should feel like help, not pressure.”  
> — SignatureHyundai Standard

---

## What this module builds
A manager turn is not a closer move.

It is a trust tool that protects:
- the guest experience
- the truth of the deal
- the salesperson’s credibility
- the momentum of the process
- the next step if the guest does not buy today

<div class="do-dont">
  <div class="dont-box">
    <span class="label-pill">Weak Manager Close</span>
    <p>Surprise entrance, pressure energy, taking over, arguing, discount chasing, or vague follow-up.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="do-box">
    <span class="label-pill">Trust-First Manager Close</span>
    <p>Clean handoff, calm tone, diagnosis, facts, two options max, one ask, and clear next step.</p>
  </div>
</div>

---

## Why it matters
When a manager turn is done poorly, the guest can feel:
- cornered
- judged
- defensive
- pushed
- less in control

When it is done well, the guest feels:
- safe
- respected
- supported
- clear on the truth
- clear on the next step
