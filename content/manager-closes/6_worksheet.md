## Manager observation checklist
<div class="coach-check">
  <p><strong>During live observation, inspect:</strong></p>
  <ol>
    <li>Was the handoff clean and expected?</li>
    <li>Did the manager diagnose before solving?</li>
    <li>Were facts used kindly?</li>
    <li>Were options limited to two?</li>
    <li>Did the manager ask once and create a clear next step?</li>
    <li>Did the consultant stay credible?</li>
  </ol>
  <p><strong>Result:</strong> Clear / Partially clear / Needs coaching</p>
</div>
