# 4.0 Scripts & Word Tracks

## 4.1 The Manager Introduction (Trust-First)
“Hi, I’m _____. I just wanted to stop in and make sure we’re taking great care of you.
Before we go further—what’s the most important thing for you to feel good about today?”

---

## 4.2 The Diagnose Question (Category Finder)
“Help me understand—are you looking for more information, or is there something that doesn’t feel comfortable yet?”

(Follow-up)
“What part feels unclear or heavy right now?”

---

## 4.3 Unshakable Facts (Calm Clarity)
“Here are the unshakable facts so this stays simple:
1) ________
2) ________
3) ________

Given that, our cleanest path is ________.”

---

## 4.4 The Two-Option Simplifier
“Based on what matters most, we really have two clean options:
- Option A: ________ (best fit)
- Option B: ________ (simpler alternative)

Which one feels closer to what you described?”

---

## 4.5 Price/Payment Pressure Release (No Intensity)
“That makes sense. Nobody wants to feel stretched.
If we can structure it in a way that feels responsible, would you feel comfortable moving forward?”

---

## 4.6 The ‘What Would Make This a Yes?’ Question
“Fair question—what would need to be true for this to feel like a ‘yes’ today?”

(Then confirm)
“Okay—so if we solve **that**, are we good?”

---

## 4.7 The Respectful Close (One Ask)
“If we can do ________, are you comfortable taking the next step with us today?”
