# 5.0 Role-Play Lab (Manager-Led)

## Setup (Groups of 3)
- Consultant
- Guest
- Observer/Coach

Rotate every 8 minutes.

---

## Drill 1: Confirm + Celebrate (10 seconds)
**Goal:** Make the yes clear and positive without hype.

Observer scores:
- Clarity (1–5)
- Warmth (1–5)
- No awkwardness (1–5)

---

## Drill 2: 3-Step Roadmap (30 seconds)
**Goal:** Deliver roadmap smoothly and confidently.

Must include:
- three steps
- time expectation
- “keep you updated” line

---

## Drill 3: Warm Handoff
Guest says:
- “So… now I have to go into finance?”
Goal:
- reframe as support
- permission-based explanation
- introduce finance as benefit, not pressure

---

## Drill 4: Anxiety Spike
Guest says:
- “I don’t know… I’m feeling nervous now.”
Goal:
- name emotion
- reassure
- reinforce fit (1 sentence)
- return to roadmap

---

## Drill 5: Waiting Room Updates
Scenario: Guest has waited 12 minutes.
Goal:
- deliver a short update
- give a new time estimate
- maintain calm tone
