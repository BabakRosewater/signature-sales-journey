## Objective
Lock in trust and momentum after commitment by guiding the guest through next steps with clarity, celebration, and zero surprises so they feel confident, cared for, and excited to take delivery.

> “People do not remember the paperwork. They remember how you made the process feel.”  
> — SignatureHyundai Standard

---

## Why this module matters
The moment the guest says yes is not the finish line.

It is a high-risk transition point.

This is where deals can get shaky because of:
- confusion about what happens next
- delays and uncertainty
- sudden tone changes
- sloppy handoffs
- surprises around fees, timing, trade details, or approval steps
- buyer’s remorse after commitment

<div class="do-dont">
  <div class="dont-box">
    <span class="label-pill">Weak After-Yes</span>
    <p>Energy drops, guest waits without updates, finance feels like pressure, handoff is unclear, or the salesperson disappears.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="do-box">
    <span class="label-pill">Strong After-Yes</span>
    <p>Confirm choice, celebrate briefly, explain three steps, set timing, hand off warmly, and keep the guest updated.</p>
  </div>
</div>
