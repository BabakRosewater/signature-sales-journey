<div class="training-card">
  <span class="label-pill">Part E • CRM Notes Checklist</span>

Circle when captured:

- Vehicle chosen + alternates
- Trade expectations: condition, payoff, items in vehicle
- Payment / term guardrails, if discussed
- Timing constraints: kid pickup, work, travel
- Delivery preferences: today/tomorrow, accessories, detail requests
- Sensitivities: anxiety, past bad experience, high urgency
</div>

## Manager observation checklist
<div class="coach-check">
  <p><strong>During live observation, inspect:</strong></p>
  <ol>
    <li>Was the yes confirmed clearly?</li>
    <li>Was there a brief, sincere celebration?</li>
    <li>Did the salesperson give a three-step roadmap?</li>
    <li>Was the handoff warm and contextual?</li>
    <li>Was the guest updated while waiting?</li>
    <li>Were CRM notes complete?</li>
  </ol>
  <p><strong>Result:</strong> Clear / Partially clear / Needs coaching</p>
</div>
